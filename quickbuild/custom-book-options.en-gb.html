Custom book options

Our standard formats, sizes and covers give you a lot of options when it comes to making your print book. But we can also help you create books as unique as your ideas.

When you want that little something extra

Looking for a way to dress up an already gorgeous book? Want to create a limited edition to sell at a premium, or use for a crowdsourced project? Perhaps you just can't imagine your book without a ribbon marker. Blurb offers five different customisation options that can be added to any book printed in volume: foil stamping, embossing and debossing, headbands and footbands, ribbon markers and custom end sheets.

Explore the options below. If you're interested in adding one or more to your book project, contact our sales team for a custom quote.

FOIL STAMPING

Foil Stamping

Foil stamping is the application of foil to paper using a heated dye. The foil adheres to the book cover surface leaving the design of the dye on the paper. Blurb offers gold (shiny and dull), silver, black or white as standard colours for print on demand projects. Other colours are available as a special order. Foil stamping is also available for offset orders in a variety of colours.

EMBOSSING/DEBOSSING

EMBOSSING/DEBOSSING

Embossing is the process of creating a raised design on the cover or spine of a book. Debossing is just the opposite: the design is sunken into the cover or spine. Blurb offers embossing on POD orders and both embossing and debossing on offset projects.

HEADBANDS & FOOTBANDS

HEADBANDS & FOOTBANDS

Headbands and footbands are narrow strips of cloth sewn or glued at the top (headband) or bottom (footband) ends of the book spine. A wide variety of colour options are available for both POD and offset projects.

Get a quote

RIBBON MARKERS

RIBBON MARKERS

Ribbon markers are strips of cloth ribbon attached to a book's headband and used as a bookmark. Ribbon markers for POD orders are gold by default, but other colours are available. A wide variety of colour choices are available for offset runs.

Get a quote

CUSTOM END SHEETS

CUSTOM END SHEETS

End sheets are the pages in the front and back of a book immediately after the front cover and before the back cover. For POD orders, solid coloured end sheets are available. For offset orders, end sheets can be solid colours or any custom single colour or four-colour design.
