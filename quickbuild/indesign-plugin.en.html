Blurb for Adobe InDesign

If you want complete creative control over your book and are comfortable using Adobe® InDesign®—the tool of choice for creative professionals—our plug-in is for you.

The book-making tool of choice for creative professionals

The Blurb Adobe InDesign plug-in lets you create and layout books and magazines directly within InDesign and output in both print and digital formats. Plug-in features include:

  • Automatic customized template creation based on your book size and page count
  • Frames for trim sizes and cut lines
  • Perfect print output with automatic error checking
  • One-click preflight and upload
  • Output of both fixed-layout and reflowable ebooks
  • Free professionally-designed starter templates for photo books, cookbooks, portfolios, and magazines

To get started, just download and install the plug-in; then choose Blurb Book Creator at the bottom of InDesign's File menu. Need more help? Review our handy guide.
