Trade books

Blurb trade books are affordably priced and can be printed in black and white or colour. They can also be published digitally in fixed layout or reflowable ebook formats. Perfect for authors of all types, trade books can be distributed and sold globally.

Choose Your Tool

Four print options, two digital formats, one amazing book – yours

The trade book format is the industry standard for authors of all types. Blurb offers three different trade sizes – 13 x 20 cm, 15 x 23 cm, and 21 x 26 cm – in both Standard and Economy options. Standard is the choice for books with lots of detailed visual elements, while Economy offers a great low-cost option for text-centric books with line art. All of our trade books can be printed in black and white or colour.

Standard Colour

White uncoated, 105 gsm (70 lb.)

A great option for books with any kind of photos, illustrations or graphics. Printing features rich, black ink and deep colours (great for page backgrounds, for example).

Economy Colour

White uncoated, 105 gsm (70 lb.)

Economy colour is a great choice if you want full colour for your photos, illustrations or graphics, but want to reduce your cost per copy.

Standard Black and White

White uncoated, 105 gsm (50 lb.)

A great choice for books that don't require colour but that have lots of illustrations, graphics or line art.

Economy Black and White

White uncoated, 75 gsm (50 lb.) or Cream uncoated, 80 gsm (50 lb.)

This option is great for books that contain mostly text and line art.

Softcover

Blurb's Trade Book softcovers are printed on the front cover, back cover and spine. The finish is gloss, and there are no flaps.

Hardcover, Imagewrap

The Trade Book ImageWrap cover choice allows you to print an image directly on the front and back, creating a smooth, sophisticated effect. ImageWrap covers feature a durable matt finish. There are no flaps.

Hardcover, Dust Jacket

This Trade Book option includes a gloss dust jacket with slate grey linen hardcover.

A novel idea: Publishing in print and digital

Dragon in the Snow is a novel inspired by the fantastic adventures of the pulp-magazine era. A tongue-in-cheek blend of two-fisted action, wry humour, and old-school science fiction, the book features a ragtag bunch of heroes, exotic locations and an unforgettable villain.

Created with Blurb's book-making tools, and published in trade softcover and as a reflowable ebook for the iPad and Kindle, Dragon in the Snow is a fine example of how Blurb's self-publishing platform supports authors from idea to publication.

Thought leadership as a business development tool

The Science of Social 2 explores how the rise of empowered customers in today's digital, social world presents an opportunity and a threat. The author, Dr. Michael Wu Ph.D, is the Chief Scientist at Lithium Technologies. Wu postulates that the survival of your business depends on how you navigate this disruption, and that the way people behave socially and commercially is, in fact, highly predictable.

By offering up a compelling, data-rich exploration of how social media can help businesses thrive, Lithium is using The Science of Social 2 not only as a way to establish thought leadership in a competitive space, but also as a cost-effective way to gain brand awareness and develop new business.

by Michael Wu, Ph.D.
