Blurb for Adobe Lightroom

The Blurb Book module is built right into the Adobe Lightroom software. Making a professional photo book is just another step in the photo management and editing workflow.

Photo editing and book-making all in one place

If you already use Lightroom to manage and edit your photos, making a Blurb book couldn't be easier. Blurb's book creation tools are fully integrated into Lightroom under the application's Book menu. Features include:

  • Over 100 customizable layouts to choose from
  • Automatic update of photos placed into book layouts
  • Caption fields with rich text formatting options
  • Five different softcover and hardcover book sizes
  • Five paper types choices, including high-end photo-quality paper from Mohawk Fine Papers®

To get started, just choose the type of online book you want to make and start creating. Need help? We've answered some of the most common Bookify "How To" questions here.
