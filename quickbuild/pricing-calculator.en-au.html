Pricing

You can make both fixed-layout and reflowable ebooks with Blurb. Fixed-layout ebooks for the iPad cost AUD $11.99. Reflowable books for the Kindle are free for a limited time. You can also create a PDF version of any book you make with Blurb for AUD $4.99.

With a wide range of papers, cover types, and trim sizes to choose from, you can make a print book in just about any configuration you can imagine. Try our Bookulator below to estimate the unit cost for a specific project you might have in mind. The Bookulator displays print on demand costs, but you can also do offset printing for bigger projects.

If you're considering a large order over 300 copies, take a look at our Volume Pricing page.
