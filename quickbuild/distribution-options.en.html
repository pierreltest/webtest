Connect with your audience

Once you've created and published your book, it's time to share and sell it. You need to choose a distribution program that meets your objectives and develop a promotion and marketing plan to connect with your audience, whoever—and wherever—they are.

Direct Sell

Sell your book directly to your fans in our online bookstore. Use your Personal Storefront widget to promote your book on your own sites and social pages. Let Blurb handle the e-commerce, shipping, customer service.

  • You keep 100% of the profit
  • Blurb handles the entire e-commerce flow and gives you tools to track your book sales
  • Sell your book with or without an ISBN
  • Sell your eBooks on Apple's iBooks Store
  • We send you profits earned via PayPal

Learn more

Sell On Amazon

Selling your book on Amazon helps you take advantage of Amazon.com's reach, recognition, and search rankings to find a wide audience.

  • You decide the list price for your book and upload your book to Blurb
  • Blurb takes care of the Amazon setup and listing
  • Blurb prints your books when they're purchased and ships the orders
  • We send you profits earned via PayPal

Learn more

Global Distribution via INGRAM

If you want to make your book accessible to online and offline book retailers globally, Blurb can help you get it listed in the Ingram Content Group catalog. Only certain book formats are eligible for the Ingram program.

  • Choose from three industry-standard sizes printed on uncoated paper: 5 x 8 in (13 x 20 cm), 6 x 9 in (13 x 20 cm), or 8 x 10 (20 x 25 cm)
  • Print in black and white or color
  • Publish hardcover and/or softcover editions

Learn more

Apple iBooks Store

Blurb makes it easy to sell your fixed-layout ebook on Apple's iBooks Store. Once you've set up your book for sale in our Bookstore, there are a few simple additonal steps to take, and we will automatically submit your book to Apple for consideration.

It can take several days for an ebook to be reviewed by Apple. If accepted, you'll receive an automated email from us notifying you that your book is now available in the iBooks Store. If your book is not approved, you will receive an automated email with Apple's reason(s). You can resubmit your book after making any required adjustments.

To learn more about getting your book in the iBooks Store, read all about it in the Blurb Knowledgebase.
