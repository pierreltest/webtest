Bookify

Bookify is the easiest way to make a photo book with Blurb. Work online – no download required – and create a book in as little as an hour.

Make a photo book in an instant (or two)

Bookify is a template-based tool that runs directly in your web browser. It's ideal for anyone who wants to make a photo book quickly and easily. Features include:

  • Online photo book creation; no download required
  • Autoflow photo import and page layout shuffle
  • Drag and drop control over images and text content
  • Integration with social media services such as Facebook and Instagram
  • Six Designer Collection 18 x 18 cm photo book templates with textured backgrounds
  • Four school photo book starter designs, each with 18 page layouts
