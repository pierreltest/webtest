Fulfilment for volume purchases

Printing a large number of books? Our warehousing and book fulfilment services can help you lower costs and raise earnings. And it can save you a lot of work, too.

Garages are for cars, not books

Large book orders don't have to mean a garage full of books and endless shipping headaches. You can lower your printing costs, manage inventory, and even speed up shipping times by printing in bulk and letting Blurb handle the warehousing and fulfilment. No garage required.

We call this service our Agile Fulfillment Programme (AFP), and it's ideal for crowd-funded book projects, authors with speaking engagements, or anyone who wants to print books in advance, in quantities of 100 copies or more. Printing in bulk lowers your costs – savings you can pass on to your readers through a lower list price. Key elements of AFP include:

  • Blurb prints the books and stores them for a nominal fee per copy
  • When orders come in, they're sent to the warehouse and can ship immediately
  • Books in the AFP are eligible for sale directly through Blurb or through the Blurb to Amazon programme
  • Books distributed via Ingram are not eligible
  • Books in inventory are insured. If any books are damaged or destroyed while in storage, we will replace them without charge
