Professionally designed book and magazine templates

You don't have to be a designer to create a professional-looking publication. Each of our free downloadable templates for BookWright and InDesign comes with 20 (or more) pages to get you started. Use the designs as they are, or customize them yourself. Choose from three different styles each for portrait and landscape photo books, magazines, cookbooks, and portfolios.
