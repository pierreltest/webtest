Volume Pricing

If you have a book or magazine project that requires printing in volume, you can take advantage of Blurb's volume pricing. And that's a big deal.

Order more, save more

Our volume pricing starts with orders as small as 20 copies. But if you're considering a project of 500 copies or more, that's where some real savings can be had. Whether you're giving your books away as gifts or promotions, crowdfunding a passion project, or selling to an established customer base, volume pricing can help to significantly reduce your overall costs.

Blurb's account management team can assist you throughout the entire publishing process. We can help you find financing options and connect you with resources you may not have yourself (e.g. cover designers and editors). During the printing process our production managers will keep you informed on the progress of your project. And we can guide you in deciding on the right warehousing, distribution, and fulfillment options.

Print on demand (POD)

For large orders ranging anywhere from 20-500 copies, POD offers a cost-effective way to get professional quality publications quickly. You can even embellish your book with our unique customization features. Our account team will help you through the entire process, from quote to delivery. Volume POD orders include:

  • A full pre-production proof copy
  • Free conversion from print to fixed-layout ebook
  • Warehousing and fulfillment options

Offset printing

For large book and magazine orders of 500 or more copies, offset printing offers a way to significantly reduce costs—up to 65% off the POD cost per unit. Any publication created in one of our many trim sizes can be printed via offset. Key benefits include:

  • A lower cost per unit
  • Customization options
  • Dedicated account management
  • Quality control with a free proof copy

Blurb offers two offset printing options: In region (US, Canada, and Europe) and offshore. Offshore printing is cheaper but will take longer. Once you approve your proof and place your order, printing and shipping takes approximately 6-10 weeks, depending on your project and where you print.
