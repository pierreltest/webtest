Print on demand (POD)

For large orders over 300 copies, POD offers a cost-effective way to get professional quality publications quickly. Our account team will guide you through the entire process, managing everything from quote to delivery. Volume POD orders include:

  • Dedicated account management
  • A full pre-production proof copy
  • Free conversion from print to fixed-layout ebook
  • Unique customization features
  • Warehousing and fulfillment options

Production time is usually 10-14 days; shipping time ranges from 4-7 days.

Offset printing

For large orders, offset printing is also an option—a good one. Offset printing can significantly reduce costs—up to 65% off the POD cost per unit. Any publication created in one of our many trim sizes can be printed via offset. Key benefits include:

  • Dedicated account management
  • A lower cost per unit
  • A wide range of customization options
  • Quality control with a free proof copy
  • Warehousing and fulfillment options

Blurb offers two offset printing options: In region (US, Canada, and Europe) and offshore. Offshore printing is often less expensive but requires more lead time. Once you approve your proof and place your order, printing and shipping takes approximately 6-10 weeks, depending on your project and where you print.
