The book-making tool of choice for creative professionals

The Blurb Adobe InDesign plug-in lets you create and lay out books and magazines directly within InDesign and output in both print and digital formats. Plug-in features include:

  • Automatic customised template creation based on your book size and page count
  • Frames for trim sizes and cut lines
  • Perfect print output with automatic error checking
  • One-click preflight and upload
  • Output of fixed-layout ebooks
  • Free professionally designed starter templates for photo books, cookbooks, portfolios and magazines

To get started, just download and install the plug-in. Then choose Blurb Book Creator at the bottom of InDesign's File menu. Need more help? Review our handy guide.
