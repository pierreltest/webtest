Professionally designed magazine and book templates

You don't have to be a designer to create a professional-looking publication; we've done the heavy lifting for you with our magazine and photo book templates. Each free downloadable file comes with 20 (or more) pages to get you started. Use the designs as they are, or customise them.

Our layout templates have been created for both BookWright and our Adobe InDesign plug-in. Templates are available for portrait and landscape photo books, magazines, cookbooks and portfolios. For each book type, you can choose from three unique template designs.
