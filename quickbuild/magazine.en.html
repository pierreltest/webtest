Magazines

Whether you're creating a monthly, a quarterly, or a year-end annual, Blurb magazines offer a serious solution to serial content. Publish in print and digital and take advantage of the design freedom the 8.5 x 11 in (22 x 28 cm) format offers.

Newsstand quality, affordably priced

Blurb magazines come in two options: Standard, printed on 60# (89 gsm) coated gloss paper with a matte cover; and Premium, printed on heavier 80# (118 gsm) matte paper with a semi-gloss cover. Both can be printed one copy at a time or in volume (with discounts beginning at just 20 copies).

You can create Blurb magazines with either our free application, BookWright, which lets you publish in print and digital formats at the same time from a single file. Or you can use our plug-in for Adobe InDesign.

Self-publishing the American Dream

Refueled is a bi-yearly publication dedicated to the exploration of community, heritage, and discovery. Founder, publisher, and creative director, Chris Brown, curates, edits, and designs each issue, incorporating the work of photographers and writers from all over the United States. The magazine's mission is to spotlight creative people pursuing the American dream and by doing so, inspire others to do the same.

Refueled is printed in Blurb's gorgeous Premium format with a semi-gloss 80# cover and matte text-stock pages on the inside. Brown has been self-publishing Refueled since 2007.
