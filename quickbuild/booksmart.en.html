BookSmart

A free downloadable application that allows you to create beautiful, customized photo books, notebooks, and planners with ease.

A book-making tool that's (almost) as smart as you are

BookSmart lets you create professional-quality books without having to hire a designer. Choose from a wide range of page layouts or create your own. Then, just add photos, text, other graphics - whatever you want. Features include:

  • Simplified layouts, background themes, and page ornaments
  • Autoflow import and placement of photos
  • Drag and drop control over images and text content
  • Templates for notebooks and planners
  • Ability to work on book projects online and offline

      • To get started, just download and install BookSmart (it's free). Launch the application, import some photos, and start making your book. Need more help? Check out the BookSmart FAQ
