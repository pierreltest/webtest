Photo Books

Whether printed on demand one book at a time, published in volume with offset printing, or distributed digitally in ebook format, photo books made with Blurb are visually stunning, professional-quality publications.

Visually rich books on photo-quality paper

From family photo books to professional portfolios, Blurb photo books are the perfect vehicles for creative expression of all kinds. Our free creation and layout tools make it possible for anyone to make a photo book in both print and digital formats.

Print book options include five different trim sizes, five photo-quality papers, three cover types, and a host of customization features. Cookbooks, graphic novels, travel guides, wedding books—whatever your book might be, it will look better if it's made with Blurb. If you're looking for a bit of inspiration, try our professionally designed starter templates for some of the most popular types of photo books.

Explore our photo book options below.

Small Square

(7 × 7 in 18 × 18 cm)

The Small Square format is great for showcasing individual photos on single pages. You can make a Small Square book with any of our creation tools, including Bookify, which lets you import photos directly from photo-rich social media sites like Facebook and Instagram. Try the Bookulator below to price your book.

Standard Portrait

(7.75 × 9.75 in 20 × 25 cm)

The Standard Portrait format just feels right when you hold it. The portrait orientation is perfect for photo books that have accompanying text. Big enough to have some presence, but not quite into the coffee table book realm. Try the Bookulator below to price your book.

Standard Landscape

(10 × 8 in 25 × 20 cm)

If you take landscape photographs, then the Standard Landscape format is a fantastic way to display them. 10 inches (25 cm) wide by 8 inches (20 cm) high, this book size is popular for travel and family photo books.

Try the Bookulator below to price your book.

Large Square

(12 × 12 in 30 × 30 cm)

There's something about a square book that brings out the creativity in design. Our Large Square format gives you an incredible canvas on which to arrange your photos and text. This is a great format for portfolios, wedding books, or just about anything.

Large Landscape

(13 × 11 in 33 × 28 cm)

If you like the Standard Landscape format, you'll love the Large Landscape. An extra three inches in both height and width give this format an incredible presence. Perfect for keepsake gift books or anything you'd display on your coffee table.

Standard

Semi-matte, 80# (118 GSM)

Our Standard paper has a smooth semi-matte finish on 80# (118 GSM) stock and is manufactured by NewPage, a company devoted to quality and sustainability. Blurb photo books can contain anywhere from 20-440 pages (that's a big book).

Premium Lustre

Hint of gloss, 100# (148 GSM)

Our Premium Lustre paper has a hint of gloss, and at 100# (148 GSM), it's about 35% heavier and is more opaque than our Standard Paper. Premium lustre paper is manufactured by NewPage, a company devoted to quality and sustainability. Blurb photo books can contain anywhere from 20-240 pages.

Premium Matte

Matte, 100# (148 GSM)

Our Premium Matte paper has a slight sheen to it, which adds richness to its finish and feel. At 100# (148 GSM), it's about 35% heavier and is more opaque than our Standard Paper, and is manufactured by NewPage, a company devoted to quality and sustainability. Blurb photo books can contain anywhere from 20-240 pages.

Proline Uncoated

Uncoated, 100# (148 GSM)

This archival-quality 100# (148 GSM) paper has an eggshell-textured, uncoated finish and is manufactured by Mohawk Fine Papers, one of the most respected makers of paper for use in photography and design books. The Mohawk name for this paper is Mohawk Superfine Eggshell Ultrawhite. Blurb photo books can contain anywhere from 20-240 pages.

Proline Pearl Photo

Semi-gloss, 140# (190 GSM)

Made for use in high-end photo books, this 140# (190 GSM) paper has a slightly glossier finish than our Premium Lustre paper and is slightly heavier than our other papers. It is manufactured by Mohawk Fine Papers, one of the most respected makers of paper for use in photography and design books (Mohawk's name for this paper is ProPhoto Lustre-Finish White). Blurb photo books can contain anywhere from 20-240 pages.

Softcover

Blurb's softcovers feature durable, perfect binding and 4-color, 10 pt. covers with an overlaminate. Printing is done on the front cover, back cover, and spine. There are no flaps.

Hardcover, Imagewrap

The ImageWrap cover choice allows you to print an image directly on the front and back of any hardcover Blurb book, creating a smooth, sophisticated effect. ImageWrap covers feature a durable matte finish. There are no flaps.

Hardcover, Dust Jacket

The hardcover dust jacket choice includes durable, library binding linen hardcover cases with laminated 4-color dust jackets. The hardcover case comes in two different cover finish options: Standard Black Linen or ProLine Linen in either Oatmeal or Charcoal.
