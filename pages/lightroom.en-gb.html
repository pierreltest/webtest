Blurb for Adobe Lightroom

The Blurb Book module is built directly into Adobe® Lightroom®. Making a professional photo book is just another step in the photo management and editing workflow.

Photo editing and book-making all in one place

If you already use Lightroom to manage and edit your photos, making a Blurb book couldn't be easier. Blurb's book creation tools are fully integrated into Lightroom under the application's Book Module. Features include:

  • Over 100 customisable layouts to choose from
  • Automatic update of photos placed into book layouts
  • Caption fields with rich text formatting options
  • Five different softcover and hardcover book sizes
  • Five paper choices, including high-end photo-quality paper from Mohawk Fine Papers®

Please note: Use of the Blurb book module requires purchasing Lightroom software directly from Adobe.
